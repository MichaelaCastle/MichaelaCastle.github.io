Overall Description

Adam is a pretty chill guy when you first meet him. He's not exactly outgoing, nor is he timid. He's just an average, nice guy. Once he gets to know someone and becomes friends with them, he opens up a bit more and starts playing around with them. He can be really funny and he also likes to tease people, but he is respectful of their boundaries. If someone tells him that they genuinely dislike when he jokes around about xyz, he'll never mention it around them again, and he expects they will do the same for him. Also, he does like to swear. It's not excessive, but he does swear.
