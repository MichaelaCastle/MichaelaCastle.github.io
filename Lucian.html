Teaser (Doubles as a starter)

It was late in the evening, the sun was starting to set. It's been days since Lucian was able to find a wild animal to draw blood from. He refuses to kill any people due to this new thirst. It's not like he was bitten by a vampire or anything, he just started noticing changes slowly... Things have been louder than normal, slower than normal. His teeth have grown fangs. And then there's the thirst... If he does nothing, this lightheaded feeling will keep getting worse. He feels weak, so weak. As a last-ditch effort, he decides to try to sneak into the hospital. Yeah, he may be stealing blood that could go to someone who might need it, but... Oh, he can't think straight, he just needs some. He's managed to sneak in to where they keep those IV blood packet things, rummaging around to find one in some drawers. From outside the room, rustling and drawers opening and closing can be heard. He can't quite see that well right now, he's a bit more clumsy than usual. Soon enough, he finds what he's looking for. Without another thought, he bites in. He doesn't even notice the door opening as someone came to investigate the sounds.

Personality

Closest Stereotype

Overall Description

Lucian often comes off as off-putting with a superiority complex. He views himself as the smartest in the room until proven otherwise, holding intelligence and knowledge in high regard. Once you get to know him, he is loyal and strangely protective. He can have a dry sense of humor, not one to express himself too much.

Likes

Ballet - He enjoys going to a lot of performances. He has multiple shows memorized

Books - He LOVES books. One of his aspirations in life is to have his own personal library, the walls decorated with nothing but books and house plants. And maybe a well-placed window to let in some natural light

Dislikes

Positive Traits

Intelligent, but not just intelligent; curious - He is always looking to learn about new things and get a better understanding of the world.

Negative Traits

Egotistical - As previously mentioned, he has a superiority complex. He believes he is the smartest in the room by default. Sure, if someone proves him wrong, he accepts that, but until then, he is a little snobby

Fears

Personal Connections - Before he even transformed, he always preferred to keep his distance and not let anyone in. But now that he's changing, he's even more scared of getting too close. He doesn't want to hurt anyone.

Hobbies

Mentoring - Lucian has a soft spot for kids and tends to take on a mentoring role around them, especially if they show an interesting in learning. He wants to encourage that curiosity and enjoys sharing his own knowledge.
