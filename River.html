Overall Description

River is known for his boundless energy and insatiable curiosity. He's never left Prismoria, but he hears stories about the outside world from his grandfather, who used to be a sailor. He always longs to understand the unknown and to explore where nobody else dares.

Normally, River is quite cheerful and optimistic. No matter what's going on, he tries to see the positive side of things.He's also an avid reader, with a particular fondness for high fantasy.
